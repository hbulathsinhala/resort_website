Wellness and Fitness

In the tranquillity of the Mandara Spa, guests can immerse themselves in the traditional healing practices of the mystical island of Bali. With 6 treatment rooms designed to harmonise body, mind and spirit, you can enjoy the rejuvenating experience of Mandara's time-honoured methods, now available to guests at the hotel.

Swimming Pools

The resort has in its facilities two exceptional infinity pools that redefine the concept of luxury and relaxation. The main pool, with a length of 37 metres and surrounded by hammocks and Balinese beds, seems to merge with the horizon, offering an incomparable view of the sunsets. In a more serene corner, the Lagoon Pool invites you to relax in an intimate setting. This 250-degree panoramic pool allows you to enjoy the impressive landscape, in a space where guests can also practice yoga or pilates.
